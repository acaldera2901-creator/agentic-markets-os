How It Works

Fully Automated

Every bet starts with data and ends with a result. Here's what happens in between.

01
📡
Collect
Live odds, team stats, historical results and player data are pulled from multiple sources every few minutes.
02
🧠
Predict
AI models calculate the true probability of each outcome — more accurately than what bookmaker odds imply.
03
⚡
Find the Edge
When our probability beats the market by a meaningful margin, a value bet signal is generated automatically.
04
🛡️
Risk Check
Stake size, exposure limits and drawdown caps are calculated and applied before any bet is placed.
05
🔄
Execute & Learn
Bet is placed on Betfair, settled automatically, and the result feeds back to make the next prediction better.
