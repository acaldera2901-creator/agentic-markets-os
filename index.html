Risk & Discipline

Unified
Cross-Desk Rules
  • Paper-first until forward track record is readable
  • Every desk produces logs, outcomes, agent health and risk config
  • Risk is normalized across markets — never copied
  • Optimizers tighten automatically; open only on clean edge
Desk-Specific
  • Football: fractional Kelly, exposure cap, completeness gate, drawdown lock
  • Crypto: 5–10% size, adaptive EV gate, stop-loss reverse engineering
  • New desks: require data source, model, risk gate and settlement before go-live
